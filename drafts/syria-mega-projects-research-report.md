## Method (5 angles + skeptic)

| Angle | Focus |
|---|---|
| **A1** | Energy & oil/gas |
| **A2** | Ports, airports, highways, logistics |
| **A3** | Real estate & urban reconstruction |
| **A4** | Telecom & digital infrastructure |
| **A5** | Open tenders / bids + cross-cutting deal-flow risk |

Every candidate finding required a **URL + date**. Two user-supplied draft reports were treated as **seed lists only** (they conflicted on ranking and over-claimed several items). Five parallel angle researchers + a skeptic pass sought contradicting, “still preliminary,” or single-sourced PR coverage. Failures were dropped or demoted. This file was **re-merged 23 Jul 2026** after late A1–A4 returns (power concessions, Tartus MHC CapEx, CMA dry ports, SilkLink Tadawul primary, Damascus airport final concession, Baramkeh early works).

**Confidence:**

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **HIGH** | Multi-source + confirmed physical / operational activity (not ceremony-only) |
| **MEDIUM** | Credible signed / awarded deal; implementation or construction not independently verified beyond company / government statements |
| **LOW** | Single-sourced, non-binding MOU, or explicitly flagged preliminary |

**Score ≈ source primacy + multi-source corroboration + operational decisiveness − opacity / preliminary flags.**

## Bottom line

**Physical / ops stack:** **DP World Tartus** (ops + Jul 2026 MHC CapEx) and **CMA CGM Latakia** (ops + AD Ports equity + dry-port expansion) remain the clearest started logistics mega-files. **Marota** (and early **Baramkeh/DFC**) are the clearest urban construction files — with HLP risk.

**Award / concession stack (not yet ops):** **SilkLink** (Tadawul-backed award), **Zain** licence, **UCC ~5 GW power concessions** (Nov 2025), **Damascus airport final concession** (early works claimed).
