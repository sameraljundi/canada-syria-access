## Survivor dossiers

### 1. Three-track compliance (SEMA ≠ AML ≠ CFT) — Confidence: High

| Finding | Source | Date | Angle | Skeptic note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| PCMLTFA Part 1 imposes client ID, record-keeping, and reporting duties on prescribed reporting entities (banks, MSBs, etc.), separate from SEMA | [Justice Laws — PCMLTFA](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/P-24.501/page-1.html) | page modified 2026-06-05 | A1 | Survives — primary statute |
| FINTRAC guidance hub is the operational AML/ATF obligation set for reporting entities | [FINTRAC guidance](https://fintrac-canafe.canada.ca/guidance-directives/guidance-directives-eng) | modified 2025-11-26 | A1 | Survives |
| Criminal Code s. 83.03 TF offences remain in force; SEMA amendments do not repeal them | [Criminal Code s. 83.03](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/c-46/section-83.03.html) | page modified 2026-06-05 | A1/A3 | Survives |
| After Feb 2026 amendments, residual Schedule 1 dealings prohibitions and chemical-weapons-related measures continue | [GAC Syria](https://www.international.gc.ca/world-monde/international_relations-relations_internationales/sanctions/syria-syrie.aspx?lang=eng) | page modified 2026-02-25; in force 2026-02-13 | A1 | Survives |

**Decision implication:** Canadian investors need a **sanctions screen**, an **AML/KYC pack for their Canadian FI**, and a **CFT/listed-entity analysis** — not one “Syria is open” checklist.

## Practical checklist for Canadian investors / businesses

1. **Define the activity** (who pays whom, which banks, which currencies/jurisdictions).  
2. **SEMA screen:** live Schedule 1 + chemical residual rules ([GAC Syria](https://www.international.gc.ca/world-monde/international_relations-relations_internationales/sanctions/syria-syrie.aspx?lang=eng); [SOR/2011-114](https://laws.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2011-114/index.html)).  
3. **CFT screen:** Criminal Code listed entities / UN-implemented lists — SEMA delist ≠ TF clearance ([Gazette SOR/2026-23](https://gazette.gc.ca/rp-pr/p2/2026/2026-02-25/html/sor-dors23-eng.html)).  
4. **KYC pack for Canadian FI:** ownership/control, purpose of funds, source of funds/wealth if PEP/high-risk ([FINTRAC](https://fintrac-canafe.canada.ca/guidance-directives/guidance-directives-eng)).  
5. **Ask the bank early** whether the corridor is processable (GAC FAQ — institutional policy).  
6. **Map US/EU/UK/FATF touchpoints** before shipping or wiring.  
7. **Escalate to counsel** for material capital, listed-person proximity, or TF-adjacent facts.
