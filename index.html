Sanctions & legal landscape

Canada eased broad economic prohibitions in 2026 while keeping targeted sanctions in place.

Key milestones

Mar 2025: General permit for selected humanitarian, stabilization, and democratization-related transactions.
Sep 2025: Permit amendments and continued guidance updates.
Feb 2026: Removal of 24 entities and one individual to ease barriers to economic activity.

What users need to know

  • Legal permission does not guarantee bankability.
  • Targeted listings and AML/CTF controls still matter.
  • Documentation and counterpart screening remain essential.
  • Cross-jurisdiction issues still arise with US/EU/UK rules.

About & methodology

Source discipline, update cadence, and transparency about AI use.
