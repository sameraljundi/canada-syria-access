## Recommended alert levels

- **Immediate:** legal amendments, new permits, new listed parties, major policy changes.
- **Weekly:** humanitarian guidance, sanctions guidance, market or sector updates.
- **Monthly:** context pages, law-firm analyses, business and investment sources.
- **Quarterly:** macro assessments and broader strategy documents.
