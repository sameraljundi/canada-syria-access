Privacy

How we handle personal information

This notice describes practices for SyriaInsight.ca in line with PIPEDA principles: identify purposes, limit collection, safeguard data, and be transparent.

Effective: 19 July 2026

What we collect

  • Support intake: email, organization type, issue type, jurisdictions, and the description you provide.
  • Optional research consent: a yes/no flag separate from receiving support.
  • Technical: standard hosting logs (IP, user agent) via our host (e.g. Vercel). We do not sell personal information.

Purposes & consent

  • Respond to your support request and improve triage quality.
  • If you opt in, use anonymized patterns for research or advocacy (never published with identifying details without separate permission).
  • Declining research consent does not reduce the support you receive.

Retention & safeguards

Intake records are retained only as long as needed for response and basic accountability (target: review within 12 months). Access is limited to operators who need it. Prefer not to send government ID images or unnecessary sensitive identifiers.

Your choices

Email hello@syriainsight.ca to request access, correction, or deletion of your intake record, subject to legal retention needs. For hosting/processor details, ask and we will identify the current providers.
