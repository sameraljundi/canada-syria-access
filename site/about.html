About & methodology

Source discipline and transparent limits

SyriaInsight.ca is a Canada-facing public research platform on Syria sanctions easing, residual compliance risk, and economic access. We prioritize Government of Canada primary sources.

Mission

Help Canadian institutions answer operational questions with clear explainers, sourced data, and lightweight tools — without pretending to replace counsel or official lists.

Source policy

  • Legal claims cite Global Affairs Canada, Justice Laws, and Canada Gazette first.
  • Law-firm notes and World Bank data are labelled as secondary context.
  • Every KPI on the site links to a primary page.
  • Material updates require a human review before production.

AI use

AI may accelerate monitoring, drafting, and triage packaging. AI does not publish compliance-sensitive guidance without human review. Automated outputs are never presented as legal clearance.

Update cadence

  • Immediate: legal amendments, new listings/delistings, major permits.
  • Weekly: guidance pages and humanitarian notes.
  • Monthly: monitor issue and figures refresh.

Disclaimer

Nothing on this website constitutes legal, financial, or tax advice. You are responsible for verifying current law and list status before acting.
