SyriaInsight.ca is operated as a public-interest research and information initiative. Unless and until we publish a Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) registration number on this page:

Canadian research and editorial standards

For public policy and legal-information content, we follow these practices aligned with Canadian public-sector source discipline and research integrity expectations:

  • Primary before secondary: Legal and sanctions facts cite Global Affairs Canada, Justice Laws, and Canada Gazette before commentary.
  • Traceable claims: Material numbers and regulatory changes include a link to the originating official page and a review date.
  • Distinguish analysis from law: Interpretive framing (for example, “bankability”) is labelled as operational context, not statutory text.
  • No undisclosed advocacy as fact: Where content supports public-interest advocacy, we keep it separate from official-source summaries.
  • Human review gate: Compliance-sensitive pages are reviewed by a human editor before production publication.
  • Corrections: If an official source changes, we update the page and the “last reviewed” date. Email corrections to hello@syriainsight.ca.
  • Content change log: Material site updates are recorded on the content change log (research transparency — not a legal bulletin).

This site does not conduct human-participant research governed by the Tri-Council Policy Statement (TCPS 2). If we later run surveys or interviews, we will disclose ethics and consent practices before collecting personal research data.

Source policy

  • Legal claims cite Global Affairs Canada, Justice Laws, and Canada Gazette first.
  • Law-firm notes and World Bank data are labelled as secondary context.
  • Every KPI on the site links to a primary page.
  • List counts are taken from official announcements as of the cited date; always re-check the live Consolidated Canadian Autonomous Sanctions List.
