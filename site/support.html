Intake for case triage

Tell us who you are, what you are trying to do, and which jurisdictions are involved. We use this to package missing facts and source links — not to provide formal legal opinions.

Submitting opens your email client to hello@syriainsight.ca (or a secure form processor once configured). See the Privacy notice for how we handle personal information.

What happens next

  1. We classify by segment, economic sector, and issue type.
  2. We identify missing facts and relevant official sources.
  3. We reply with an informational triage note, or suggest that you retain independent counsel when needed.

Response expectations

Launch phase: best-effort reply within several business days. This is not a crisis hotline, legal clinic, or guaranteed service. We do not issue donation receipts or act as a registered charity on this form.

Do not send

Passwords, government identity document images, health records, or classified material. Share only what is needed for triage.
